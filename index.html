What is Shilajit?

Harvested from the towering heights of the Himalayas, it takes decades for organic biological matter to be crushed and trapped between rocks at the highest elevations on earth. At these specific atmospheric conditions, the fermented medicinal plants become the raw material, wonder of nature, known as shilajit. These rocks are naturally gathered from mountain crevices, purified, then artisanally made into shilajit resin in adherence to the highest of standards. As a powerhouse of nutrients, natural elements, and biologically active organic substances, Himoganic's Shilajit is crafted to be the most exclusive ancient super nutrient designed for health and longevity. Infused with the strength of the mountains, it’s a treasure trove of minerals and goodness. Elevate your wellness, naturally.
